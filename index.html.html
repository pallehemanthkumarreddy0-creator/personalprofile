About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest modern-day cricketers and a former captain of the Indian national cricket team. Known for his consistency, aggressive batting style, and strong leadership, he has broken numerous records across all formats of cricket.

He is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the history of international cricket and is famous for his ability to chase targets successfully.
