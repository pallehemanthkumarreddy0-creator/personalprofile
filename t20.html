Player Profile

Virat Kohli is one of the greatest modern-day cricketers and former captain of the Indian cricket team. He is known for his aggressive batting style, incredible consistency, and ability to chase targets in limited overs cricket.

  • Full Name: Virat Kohli
  • Date of Birth: November 5, 1988
  • Birthplace: Delhi, India
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Role: Top order batsman
  • International Debut: 2008
